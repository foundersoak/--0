# Attribution & data sources

**Flawless Lineup** is an unofficial fan game. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by
the NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, NCAA, the Premier League, or any team. Team names and colors are used
nominatively to identify franchises; no team logos or player likenesses are used.

## Player data

Player records are factual statistics compiled at build time from permissively-available open
datasets and committed into the repo. Individual statistical facts are not copyrightable. We do
**not** scrape or redistribute Sports-Reference data.

- **NBA**, [peasant98/TheNBACSV](https://github.com/peasant98/TheNBACSV) (Kaggle "NBA Seasons Stats",
  1950-2017), [Brescou/NBA-dataset-stats-player-team](https://github.com/Brescou/NBA-dataset-stats-player-team)
  (1996-2023), and [NocturneBear/NBA-Data-2010-2024](https://github.com/NocturneBear/NBA-Data-2010-2024)
  (2010-2024). Pre-1974 steals/blocks are imputed (not tracked then).
- **MLB**, the [Lahman Baseball Database](https://sabr.org/lahman-database/) (CC BY-SA 3.0).
- **NFL**, [nflverse](https://github.com/nflverse) open data.
- **NHL**, [MoneyPuck](https://moneypuck.com/data.htm) and open Kaggle datasets.
- **Soccer**, [StatsBomb Open Data](https://github.com/statsbomb/open-data).

CC BY-SA-derived datasets, where used, are kept in clearly marked files and shared under the same
license; this obligation does not extend to the application code.
